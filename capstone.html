Welcome to my Untitled Capstone project I am currently working on in the MPS Communication Design program. The project is divided into 3 parts: Research, Prototyping, and finally UX/UI Design, and the duration is 16 weeks.

My focus is on the topic of organization and through research, exploratory interviews, and extracting insights, I narrowed down what people want when it comes to organizing and tidying up physical spaces.
